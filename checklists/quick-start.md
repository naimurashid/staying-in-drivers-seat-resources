# Quick-start — 5 actions, adopt in order

If you saw the talk and something on the [calibration list](calibration-questions.md) got a "no" — start here.

## In order

1. **Open a `DECISIONS.md` in your active project this week.** Use the [template](../templates/DECISIONS.md).
2. **Before your next AI prompt, write your own approach first.** Use the [Pre-Prompt Pause checklist](pre-prompt-pause.md).
3. **Schedule one 90-minute unplugged session this week.** Pick a real problem; do it without AI.
4. **Line-by-line review on the next AI-generated chunk.** Don't ship what you can't explain.
5. **Explain-it-back on the last thing you built with AI help.** Use the [3-criteria check](explain-it-back.md).

## The rule

**Adopt one. Add the next when the first feels routine.**

## A year in

You can defend your work without your notes; your trainees can defend theirs.
